Cheeseburgers

Description

My favorite food growing up. This is a very simple recipe that is good for beginners.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • Burger Patty
  • Burger Bun
  • Cheese of your choice
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Tomato (Optional)

Steps

  1. Place a pan on a stovetop. Lubricate the pan with olive oil, and set the stovehead underneath this pan to medium heat.
  2. Once the pan reaches an appropriate temperature, place the burger patty on the pan. Flip the patty every one to two minutes.
  3. (Optional) In the meantime, toast your burger buns to your liking.
  4. Use an instant-read thermometer to periodically measure the burger's temperature. It will be safe to consume after it has reached 145° F, but you can continue cooking it to your liking after this stage.
  5. Before you decide to take the patty off, add the cheese so it meltsnicely once you're done cooking the patty.
  6. When the patty is done cooking, place it on top of your bun. Place tyour toppings on top of your bun.
