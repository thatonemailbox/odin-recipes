Homemade Potato Chips

Adapted from this recipe from Food Network. Note that this recipe is being used only for educational purposes, with respect to demonstrating how an html site is constructed. I am in no way claiming that this recipe is my own.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds potatoes
  • 2 teaspoons garlic salt
  • 2 teaspoons paprika
  • 2 teaspoons black pepper
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • Vegetable oil of choice for frying

Steps

  1. Scrub the potatoes under water until totally clean.
  2. Use a mandolin or a Japanese slicer to slice the potatoes as thinly as possible. As you slice them, submerge them in cold water and set aside. Lay the potatoes on a baking sheet lined with paper towels, and blot them with more paper towels to dry completely.
  3. Mix together the garlic salt, paprika, black pepper, and salt in a small bowl and set aside.
  4. In 2 heavy Dutch ovens, heat some vegetable oil to 375° F, while using a thermometer to check its temperature regularly.
  5. When the oil is ready, lower batches of the potatoes into the oil using a spatula. They will fry in about 11⁄2 minutes. Remove the batch with a spatula when they're nice and golden and crisp. Immediately afterwards, sprinkle on the spice mix. Repeat with the rest of the potatoes.
